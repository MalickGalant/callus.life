Here are some key features of TikTok:

Voiceover - An exciting feature of this application is the ability to record voiceovers for your videos.

Music integration - Users can add background music to their videos, with a vast library of popular songs and sound bites to choose from.

Editing tools - This app provides a range of editing tools to customize videos, including filters, effects, text overlays, and stickers.

Duets and collaborations - Users can create duets and collaborations with other users by combining videos and adding a split-screen.

Hashtags and challenges - This platform popularized the use of hashtags and challenges, where users can participate in trending topics or create their challenges.

Discover feed - The Discover Feed in this app showcases trending videos and popular creators, providing a platform for users to find and follow content creators.

Algorithmic feed - TikTok's algorithmic feed provides users with personalized video recommendations based on their viewing habits and interests.

Live video streaming - This feature allows you to broadcast live videos where your followers and non-followers can interact with you in real time.
